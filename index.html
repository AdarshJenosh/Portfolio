About Me

Hi there, I’m Adarsh , a passionate UI/UX designer with a B.Tech Computer Science degree , blending technical knowledge with creative problem-solving to craft intuitive and impactful digital experiences.

My background in computer science gives me a strong foundation in understanding how things work behind the scenes, which helps me design user interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also functional and developer-friendly.

I specialize in designing clean, user-centered interfaces for web and mobile platforms, and I thrive on turning complex ideas into simple, accessible designs.Whether it's wireframing, prototyping, or collaborating with developers, I bring a detail-oriented and user-first mindset to every project I work on and is dedicated to I'm continuously learning, experimenting, and refining my skills to stay aligned with evolving design trends and user needs.

Recent Works

MediLink – A Modern Telehealth App Design

MediLink is a mobile healthcare app designed to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers through a seamless digital experience. The project focuses on creating a clean, accessible, and intuitive interface that simplifies appointment booking, virtual consultations, medical records management, and ongoing patient-doctor communication.

CoffeeCorner – Mobile App design for Seamless Coffee Ordering

CoffeeCorner is a modern coffee shop app designed to bring the cozy café experience right to your fingertips. This design captures the essence of artisan coffee culture—warm, inviting, and convenient. From ordering your favorite brew to discovering new blends and managing loyalty rewards, the app offers an all-in-one digital café experience.

SmartBill – Simplified Bill Payment App Design

SmartBill is a modern mobile app that simplifies online bill management by offering a minimal, intuitive design. Built to support fast, secure payments, it provides users with quick access to all essential services, a smart reminder system, and integrated payment functionality—all in one place.

Atlas Aura – Travel Booking Website Design

Atlas Aura is a modern travel booking platform designed to elevate the user's journey from discovery to booking. The goal was to create an immersive, visually stunning experience that inspires travelers while making trip planning easy and intuitive.

Aeternime - The Ultimate Anime Experience Hub App

Aeternime is a unified anime hub designed to seamlessly blend anime streaming, manga and webtoon reading, and exclusive merchandise shopping. It offers fans a deeply immersive experience by centralizing all their favorite content and products in one elegant, user-friendly platform.

Executive Panel - Analytical Dashboard

A centralized, high-level interface designed for executives and decision-makers to monitor business performance, track key metrics, and analyze real-time data. This dashboard offers clear visual insights into projects, employee activity, financial trends, and operational efficiency — empowering leaders to make informed, strategic decisions with ease.
